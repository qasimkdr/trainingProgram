How It Works

🚀 Step 1: Class Selection

Click the "Auto-Schedule" button and select a class. The system fetches its branch and term automatically.

🧠 Step 2: Subject Detection

Based on the branch and term, relevant subjects are pulled from the database. Only valid instructors are shown.

🤖 Step 3: AI Instructor Assignment

Assign instructors to each subject and the tool ensures no one exceeds their max limit. Smart alerts included!

📋 Step 4: Final Schedule

View or export the final dynamic schedule. You can edit, tweak, or regenerate with ease!
